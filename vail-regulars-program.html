How Vail Regulars Works

No points to track, no cards to carry. Just book, stay, and watch your discount grow.

STEP 01

Book & Stay

Reserve any rentVAIL home in Vail or Beaver Creek. Every completed stay counts toward your membership tier.

STEP 02

Climb the Tiers

As your completed stays add up, you move from Silver to Bronze, Gold, and Platinum, each with a bigger discount.

STEP 03

Call or Email to Apply

To apply your member discount to a stay, just call or email our team and we will add it to your booking based on your current tier.

Good To Know

Program Details

The Fine Print

  • Tier status is based on stays completed within an 18-month period.
  • Your membership window resets 18 months after your first qualifying stay.
  • Blackout dates apply: during New Year's week and Presidents' week, a maximum discount of 10% applies regardless of your tier.
  • To apply your member discount to a stay, call or email our team and we will add it to your booking.
  • Reaching Platinum unlocks 20% off every stay for the following months within the 18-month period.
Questions

Frequently Asked

How do I join Vail Regulars?

Membership begins automatically after your first completed stay. To get set up or learn more, call our team at 970-476-9322.

How do I get my discount applied?

Once you're a member, call or email our team and we will apply your discount to your stay based on your current tier.

Does my discount work on all properties?

Yes. Your member rate applies to every rentVAIL home in Vail and Beaver Creek.

What happens if I don't stay for a while?

Your tier is based on stays within an 18-month window and resets 18 months after your first qualifying stay, so keep returning to keep climbing.
