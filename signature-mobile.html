Install on iPhone (Apple Mail)

  1. Open this file on the phone (email it to yourself, or open in Safari). Press and hold inside the box, Select All, Copy.
  2. Go to Settings → Apps → Mail → Signature (older iOS: Settings → Mail → Signature).
  3. Clear the field, press and hold, tap Paste. Send a test to confirm.
